Not your typical Restaurant Ordering System

Raccoon Express was created to provide a system that coordinates all aspects of daily restaurant management and operations. The activities that typically constitute a transaction at a restaurant are integrated to create seamless and efficient transfer of data between the different groups involved. At peak times, restaurants can often become very hectic, putting stress on staff, increasing risk of error, and potentially degrading customer experience. Raccoon Express alleviates much of the overhead involved in every transaction at a restaurant, allowing staff to focus on improving customer experience, and clients to receive the most efficient and pleasant experience possible.
